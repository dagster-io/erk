# Onboarding

## How to get your organization set up with Compass

### Step 1: Create your account

- Follow the link in your email to create your account
- Enter the email you use to sign in to Slack
- Choose a name for your organization
- Create your account

> **⚠️ To connect Compass to your organization’s Slack, you’ll need permission to accept Slack Connect invitations. If you don’t have access, your Slack admin may need to complete this part of the onboarding process.**
>
> [Learn about Slack Admin Setup with Compass](/admins/slack-admin-setup)

### Step 2: Accept Slack Connect invite

- Check your email and accept the Slack Connect invite
